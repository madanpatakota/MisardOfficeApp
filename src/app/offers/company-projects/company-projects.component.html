Nyayabharth
India’s Legal Knowledge Platform

Nyayabharth empowers Indian citizens to understand the law — whether you're a law student, civil aspirant, or everyday citizen.

  • 511 IPC Sections + 484 CrPC Sections explained in simple language
  • Real-life legal examples and punishments
  • Mobile-first app with offline search & PDF export
  • Admin CMS, APIs, and user feedback system
Angular .NET Core Azure
Agrivikas
Empowering Indian Farmers

Agrivikas is a digital farming assistant that provides rural farmers with real-time agricultural guidance.

  • Live mandi prices & voice assistant (Telugu)
  • Crop-specific advice, soil health tips & weather updates
  • Offline access and rural network optimization
  • Farm expert integration for question/answer chat
Angular Node.js Firebase
SmartLife
Productivity for Everyday Living

SmartLife is a lifestyle productivity app that blends technology and mental wellness to streamline your day.

  • Pomodoro timers, daily planning, goal setting
  • Habit tracking, voice notes & multilingual UI
  • IoT reminders integrated with smart devices
  • Capacitor-based mobile-first architecture
Angular Firebase Capacitor
